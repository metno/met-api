Example Requests

The following are URIs for a few common requests. These are similar to the example requests for the wsKlima API.

1. Data from the climate stations identified by climate numbers 18700 and 50540 for august and september only, for three years (2004 - 2006).
https://data.met.no/observations/v0.jsonld?sources=SN18700,SN50540&referencetime=R3/2004-08-01T00:00/2004-10-01T00:00/P1Y&elements=max(air_temperature P1M),min(air_temperature P1M),sum(precipitation_amount P1M)

2. Monthly means for max and min temperatures as well as accumulated precipitation for all months from July 2005 to July 2006.
https://data.met.no/observations/v0.jsonld?sources=SN18700,SN50540&referencetime=2005-07-01T00:00/2006-07-01T00:00&elements=max(air_temperature P1M),min(air_temperature P1M),sum(precipitation_amount P1M)

3. Observations for the 0, 6, 12, and 18 hours UTC for the given period.
https://data.met.no/observations/v0.jsonld?sources=SN18700:0&referencetime=R186/2006-01-01T00:00:00.000Z/2006-01-01T00:00:00.000Z/PT6H&elements=mean(air_temperature PT1H),max(air_temperature PT1H)

4. Monthly means (not normals) over a 30 year period.
https://data.met.no/observations/v0.jsonld?sources=SN18700,SN50540&referencetime=1961-01-01T00:00/P30Y&elements=mean(air_temperature P1M)

5. Min and max temperatures observed from Gardermoen airport (SN4780). Metar data is not available yet.
https://staging-data.met.no/observations/v0.jsonld?sources=SN4780:0&referencetime=2008-09-01T00:00:00.000Z/2008-10-01T00:00:00.000Z&elements=min(air_temperature PT12H),max(air_temperature PT12H)

6. As above, but the 06:00 observations only
https://staging-data.met.no/observations/v0.jsonld?sources=SN4780:0&referencetime=R30/2008-09-01T06:00:00.000Z/2008-09-01T06:00:00.000Z/PT24H&elements=min(air_temperature PT12H),max(air_temperature PT12H)

7. Environmental data observed at 1200 from Ekofisk oil platform (SN76920). Note the presence of a hole in the data (no available measurement on the 14th september)
https://data.met.nohttps://observations/v0.jsonld?sources=SN76920&referencetime=R30/2008-09-01T12:00/2008-09-01T12:00/PT24H&elements=max(air_temperature PT1H),min(air_temperature PT1H)

8. 10-minute precipitation observations for 12 UTC during the month of may, 2016 at climate station Blindern:
https://data.met.no/observations/v0.jsonld?sources=SN18700:0&referencetime=R31/2016-05-01T12:00:00.000Z/2016-05-01T12:00:00.000Z/PT24H&elements=sum(precipitation_amount PT10M)

9. All of the 10-minute precipitation observations at climate station Blindern for the month of may, 2016
https://data.met.no/observations/v0.jsonld?sources=SN18700:0&referencetime=2016-05-01T00:00:00.000Z/2016-06-01T00:00:00.000Z&elements=sum(precipitation_amount PT10M)

10. All available time series from Ekofisk (SN76920) (not the actual observations):
https://data.met.no/observations/availableTimeSeries/v0.jsonld?sources=SN76920

11. All values of max wind speed of gust observed at 10-minute intervals at Ekofisk during a certain time period:
https://data.met.no/observations/v0.jsonld?sources=SN76920:0&referencetime=2007-01-26T06:00:00.000Z/2013-11-30T13:00:00.000Z&elements=max(wind_speed_of_gust PT10M)

12. IDF data for Oslo Blindern:
https://data.met.no/frequencies/rainfall/v0.jsonld?sources=SN18701
